Common Questions

Frequently Asked

How long does a typical project take?
+
Project timelines vary significantly by scope. A simple landing page or MVP might take 4–8 weeks. A full-featured web application typically takes 3–6 months. Enterprise platforms can take 6–18 months. We'll give you a detailed timeline estimate after our initial discovery conversation.
What does a project with ITCONIC cost?
+
Our project budgets start at $15,000 for focused engagements and scale to $500,000+ for large enterprise programs. We offer fixed-price engagements for well-scoped projects and time-and-materials for more exploratory work. We'll recommend the right model for your situation.
Do you sign NDAs?
+
Absolutely. We're happy to sign a mutual NDA before you share any confidential project details. Your ideas and business information are always protected. Just let us know and we'll send over our standard NDA for review.
How do you handle project communication and updates?
+
You'll have a dedicated project manager as your single point of contact. We work in 2-week sprints with live demos at the end of each sprint. Daily async updates in Slack/Teams, weekly status calls, and a real-time dashboard so you always know exactly where things stand.
Do you provide ongoing support after launch?
+
Yes. We offer flexible support and maintenance packages ranging from a 3-month warranty period (included in all projects) to ongoing managed services with 24/7 monitoring, regular updates, and proactive performance optimization.
Can you work with our existing team?
+
Absolutely. Many of our engagements are collaborative — we embed with client engineering teams, provide specialist expertise, and work within your existing processes and tools. We're happy to work as a dedicated agency or as an extension of your in-house team.
