Our Story

Born From Passion,
Driven by Purpose

ITCONIC was founded in 2016 by a group of engineers who believed technology should be more than functional — it should be transformative. Starting as a small web development shop in San Francisco, we've grown into a full-service technology partner serving clients across 45+ countries.

Today, we stand at the intersection of design, engineering, and artificial intelligence. Every project we take on is an opportunity to push boundaries, challenge assumptions, and deliver something the world hasn't seen before.
